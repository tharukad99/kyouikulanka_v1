    JLPT & NAT

    Japanese Spoken Class

    Course Overview:
    his course focuses on improving spoken Japanese for daily communication. It is designed for students who want to speak fluently and confidently in real-life situations, whether for study, work, or living in Japan.

    What You Will Learn:
    • Greetings & Introductions: Learn essential greetings and how to introduce yourself confidently.
    • Everyday Conversation: Practice communication for shopping, travel, dining, school, and workplace situations.
    • Speaking Styles: Use both polite and casual Japanese appropriately.
    • Listening Skills: Train your ear with natural Japanese speech and real-life dialogues.
    • Pronunciation Drills: Improve clarity and fluency through guided speaking practice.
    • Role-Plays: Engage in interactive role-plays and conversations with instructors.

    Course Features:
    • 100% Practical: Focus entirely on conversation-based learning.
    • Grammar Made Simple: Speak without memorizing long grammar rules first.
    • Interactive Sessions: Develop fluency through engaging speaking practice.
    • Audio & Video Resources: Reinforce learning with practical materials.
    • Real-Life Focus: Emphasis on communication skills, not just exams.

    Who Can Join:
    • Complete Beginners: Start speaking Japanese from day one.
    • Intermediate Learners: Improve fluency and confidence in real situations.
    • Future Residents & Travelers: Perfect for anyone planning to live, work, or travel in Japan.
