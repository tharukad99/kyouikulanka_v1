Our Main Courses

JLPT

Structured training for all levels (N5–N1). Focus on grammar, vocabulary, reading, and listening to ensure success in the official JLPT exams.

NAT

Comprehensive preparation for the NAT test, widely accepted in Japan for study and work opportunities. Covers practical language skills and exam strategies.

JFT

Ideal for those applying for Specified Skilled Worker (SSW) visas. Focuses on everyday communication skills and cultural understanding.

Spoken

Practical conversational Japanese for daily life, travel, and workplace situations. Perfect for beginners and those who want to improve fluency.

Visa Types We Support

Student Visa

For students enrolling in Japanese language schools, universities, or vocational colleges.

Specified Skilled Worker (SSW)

Work visa for skilled workers in industries facing labor shortages in Japan.

Training Visa

For technical interns and trainees who want to gain hands-on skills in Japan.

Job Visa

Professional visa for full-time employment in companies across various industries.

Dependent Visa

For family members (spouse, children) of individuals residing in Japan on other visas.

Business Manager Visa

Designed for entrepreneurs and managers to establish or operate businesses in Japan.
